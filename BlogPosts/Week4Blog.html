Goal alignment

As explained in the previous blog, my goal is to create a website that caters first-class treatment to users that visit it. This will be achieved through an accessible, cohesive and visually refined website that encapsulates sophistication and elegance. This choice is largely influenced by my interest in haute couture fashion culture which is deeply inspiring my approach to designing this website. My website should feel warm and welcoming with an interesting design to it that embodies a sophisticated and high fashion-tone to it. This will be reflected in every webpage having a carefully curated colour palette, intentional font style and appealing visuals to enhance the redefined aesthetic. Also, there is a well-structured UI that ensures seamless navigation - a huge part of first-class experience is ensuring that users don’t have to hustle for anything.

Additionally, I will incorporate a strong information hierarchy and a polished layout which enhances readability, making content inviting and effortless to consume which will make my users feel called to interact with my site. The reason behind this precision in my IxD process is because my target audience is companies in the animation and Interactive Media industry, as well as artists seeking inspiration. I don’t want my website to be ordinary, it should be an experience that rejuvenates creativity and sparks inspiration. This vision should be maintained all while interacting with subtle class and elegance to it. Over and above this, my platform should be accessible, so a part of my goal is ensuring that my website can work on different devices, so that’s ensuring that my specifications are diverse and adoptable.

Your Content

The content of my website will include a homepage, blogs posts, essays, portfolio work and to show my personality, a profile will be included along with links to webpages where information about me will be displayed (Instagram, Pinterest, LinkedIn etc). I also will have a section dedicated to my design process, this will show that I have the skillset to plan, implement and execute. I think the most important aspects in my content is maintaining consistency and simplicity. This decision is also tied to the objective of ensuring that everyone with basic knowledge can navigate and operate the function on my site. It shouldn’t feel like a person in tech is the only person who can explore it. I don't want my users to be overwhelmed by this experience, everything should be simple with a professional tone to it. What is important and matters most to me is ensuring that my website is up to standard and has an artistic flair to it.

Information Structure - Content Mapping

Consistent information that has to be on every webpage is the navigation bar and footer. I think another aspect is ensuring that every page vividly outlines the page title so that my user is aware of their location within my website. The interface elements I will be using consist of: buttons, search field, icons, text field modal windows and images.

Design - Style Guide & Wireframes

I want my website to look and feel elegant, classy, chic and sophisticated. This is obviously related to the haute couture culture that I want my website to embody. I want the Didot font for my headings and the Avenir font for my body texts. These fonts also have good readability and legibility. I want my colour palette to consist of muted and neutral tones, these sell an atmosphere of class and elegance. They also have a modern feel to them. Most shapes on my website will be circular or curved to give off that feminine energy.
