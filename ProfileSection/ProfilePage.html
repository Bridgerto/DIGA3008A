PROFILE SECTION

Hi there! My name is Reneilwe Thobejane. I'm a 20-year-old Digital Artist, currently navigating my third year of majoring in Animation and Interactive Media. I spend most of my days making things move, respond, and occasionally glitch in oddly satisfying ways. This website is the product of many late nights spent poking around code, learning how to bring ideas to life through HTML, CSS, JavaScript and a lot of “why isn't this centering?” moments. Think of this space as a growing portfolio and a kind of digital journal, showcasing the work I've done across my degree, while also dipping my toes into what it means to build a home for my creative practice online.
While fashion doesn't take centre stage here (yet!), it's a huge inspiration behind how I approached designing the layout and aesthetic of this site. I'm fascinated by the visual language of fashion sites, from the sleek grids, bold typography, editorial flair, and you might spot some of that influence sprinkled throughout. More than anything, this site is me learning in public: the experimenting, building, and figuring things out one animated hover effect at a time. Welcome to my digital corner of chaos and curiosity!
